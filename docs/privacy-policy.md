# UNQX Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-03-07
**Effective date:** 2026-03-07

## 1. General
UNQX is a digital business card service available at https://unqx.uz.
This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## 2. Data we collect
### 2.1 Data you provide
- Name
- Phone number
- Email
- Telegram username
- Profile photo (optional)
- Job and company details
- Social links

### 2.2 Data collected automatically
- Card view and tap counts
- Source of view (NFC, QR, direct link)
- Approximate city by IP (not precise location)
- Device and browser type
- Timestamp of interactions

### 2.3 Device/app data
- Push token (with permission)
- Device/session identifiers for security

### 2.4 Data we do not collect directly
- Exact geolocation
- Phonebook contacts
- Raw biometric data
- Bank card details

## 3. How we use data
- Provide your digital card profile
- Provide analytics
- Send notifications (if enabled)
- Improve product reliability and support

## 4. Sharing
We do not sell personal data.
Data may be shared only with:
- payment processors (Payme, Click) when required for transactions
- error monitoring providers (for crash diagnostics)
- push infrastructure (for notifications)
- authorities when legally required

## 5. Storage
- Data is stored on secured infrastructure
- Analytics retention may be limited by business policy
- Account data is retained while account is active and for a limited period after deletion request

## 6. Your rights
You can request data access, correction, or deletion.
Contact: privacy@unqx.uz

## 7. Security
- Secure session/token storage
- HTTPS/TLS transport
- Password hashing on backend

## 8. Children
Service is intended for adults (18+).

## 9. Policy changes
Material updates are announced in advance where feasible.
